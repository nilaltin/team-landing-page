> **Project Charter Template**

**Project Name:** Pacific Northwest Hiking Web App (The Great Outdoors)

**Project Sponsor:** Evan Holt, Founder – The Great Outdoors

1.  Project Purpose / Background

> *(Why this project is being undertaken; business need or problem it
> addresses)*

This project is being undertaken to create a centralized, modern, and
user-friendly web application that provides accurate and up-to-date
hiking trail information for the Pacific Northwest. Currently, trail
information is scattered across outdated websites, social media posts,
and PDFs, making it difficult for hikers to find reliable and consistent
information.

The Great Outdoors aims to address overcrowding on popular trails,
promote safety and seasonal awareness, and encourage responsible outdoor
recreation through environmental stewardship. By launching a centralized
web application, the organization can better serve beginner and
experienced hikers while supporting long-term growth, community
building, and partnerships.

1.  Project Objectives

> *(What the project aims to achieve; should be specific and measurable
> if possible)*
>
> ■ Objective 1:

Develop and launch a functional Minimum Viable Product (MVP) hiking web
app within 2–4 months.

> ■ Objective 2:

Provide reliable trail information including difficulty, distance,
elevation gain, and safety notes.

> ■ Objective 3:

Promote safe and responsible hiking practices while highlighting
lesser-known trails.

Success Criteria

- MVP launched on time within the agreed schedule

- Users can easily browse trails and view accurate trail details

- Positive feedback from early users on usability and clarity

1.  Scope

> **In Scope:**

- Public-facing hiking website (desktop-first, mobile-friendly)

- Trail listings with difficulty, distance, elevation gain

- Safety and seasonal awareness content

- Highlighting lesser-known trails

- Basic community features (reviews or comments; limited)

- Simple admin content management (if feasible) **Out of Scope:**

- Advanced mobile app (native iOS/Android)

- Complex GIS or real-time trail condition tracking

- Large-scale social networking features

- Full e-commerce system

- Advanced analytics and AI-based recommendations

1.  High-Level Deliverables

> *(Key outputs/products of the project)*
>
> ■ Deliverable 1: Functional MVP Hiking Web Application
>
> ■ Deliverable 2: Trail database with curated Pacific Northwest trails
>
> ■ Deliverable 3: Basic documentation and user guidance for the client
